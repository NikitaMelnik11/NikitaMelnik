About Me

My name is Nikita Melnyk and my favorite number is 3. I live in Ukraine and I'm 16 years old. I've decided to take up basketball and aim for the NBA draft. You might think it's funny, but look at Dennis Rodman who started seriously at the age of 21. Joel Embiid just started playing basketball at 15. Steven Adams decided to play basketball at 16.

What I'm getting at is there's always a chance to show what you live for and what you want to do. I want to be a professional basketball player. I've been doing it for a long time. I've been training for it for a long time, so there's something left in me. Remember my first and last name and you'll see it on the draft board, I promise you that.

Every day, I dedicate myself to honing my skills on the court, pushing my limits, and striving to become the best player I can be. The rhythm of the bouncing ball, the swish of the net, and the energy of the game fuel my determination to succeed in this sport I love so deeply.
